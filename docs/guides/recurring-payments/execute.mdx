Recurring payouts are collected through the same contract-backed claim model used elsewhere in FlowGuard. The app or an executor can trigger the due recurring window, but the covenant still enforces the cadence and amount.

## In the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recurring payment or recurring stream">
    Navigate to the payment or recurring stream detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check what is due">
    The detail page shows the cadence, the current due amount, and whether the schedule is
    refillable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim">
    Click **Claim**. The app requests the transaction from the backend and the recipient wallet
    signs it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    After broadcast, FlowGuard confirms the claim and updates recurring state, history, and
    activity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the Transaction Does

A valid recurring claim:

1. pays the amount due to the fixed recipient
2. advances the recurring window
3. updates total paid state
4. keeps the recurring state UTXO alive when the schedule continues

## Refillable Runway

If the recurring schedule is refillable:

- the sender can add more runway later
- the recurring claim flow stays the same
- the stream detail page will show refill actions when the sender is connected
